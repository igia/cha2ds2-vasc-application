Welcome to the CHA2DS2-VASc Score Calculator

A sample application as part of the igia. This program calculates the annual risk of stroke for someone with non-valvular atrial fibrillation based on a variety of risk factors. The application demonstrates how to use the platform to build a front end application and a microservice that are both integrated into the overall platform workflow.
